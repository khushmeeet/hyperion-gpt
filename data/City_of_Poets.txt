City of Poets
The City of Poets was a city on the planet Hyperion to the north of the Bridle Range on the continent Equus. It was the first city constructed on the planet during it's second wave of colonization and the closest city to the Time Tombs. At the time of the first Shrike murders, its population was 6,000.[1] The city was evacuated after the first Shrike murders. In the time of the Final Shrike Pilgrimage the city is abandoned.
