Jamnu Republic
The Jamnu Republic is a country or region of the planet of Deneb Drei.  [1] Corporal Bassin Kee was from the Jamnu Republic.[2]
 In Martin Silenus's multi-world house, the tower study had a three-hundred-sixty-degree view of the highest peaks of the Kushpat Karakoram, a mountain range two thousand kilometers from the nearest settlement in the easternmost reaches of the Jamnu Republic on Deneb Drei.[3]
