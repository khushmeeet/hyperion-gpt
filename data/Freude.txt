Freude
Freude was a sunny planet that was part of the WorldWeb during the Web Era. It was part of the River Tethys circuit.  The days on Freude were thirty-nine standard hours long. Fiberplastic was cultivated there. 
 Its inhabitants wore colorful clothes of harlequin fabrics and brightly colored capes. During the Pax Era, it was a Pax world. During that period, it was a bustling, heavily populated world.
