Hyperion (disambiguation)
In the Hyperion universe, Hyperion most frequently refers to the planet Hyperion. The planet Hyperion is the location of the Time Tombs, the opening of which form the catalyst for the Hyperion saga. 
Hyperion may also refer to the following:

Hyperion (moon) - The moon of Saturn, from which the planet Hyperion is named and from where the original settlers of the planet Hyperion originated.
Hyperion (novel) - The first of the four novels set in the Hyperion universe.
Hyperion (poem) - The unfinished epic poem by John Keats.
Hyperion (Titan) - The Greek mythological giant and namesake of Keats' poem.
