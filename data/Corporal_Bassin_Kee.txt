Corporal Bassin Kee
Corporal Bassin Kee is a Pax military officer and a supporting character in Endymion and The Rise of Endymion novels, appearing alongside Father Captain Federico de Soya, Sergeant Gregorius and Lancer Rettig. He is from the Jamnu Republic on Deneb Drei.[1]
Kee is a small man, compact and quick in both reflexes and intelligence.
