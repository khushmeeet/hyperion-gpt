Mao Four
A non-Christian world identified by Cardinal Du Noyer as one from which all non-born-again Christians were placed into a state of stasis by the TechnoCore and transferred by Opus Dei to holding within one of the labyrinthine worlds. Over the course of seven years, the number transferred in this manner, combined with 17 other worlds, totals approximately seven billion individuals.[1]
