Stables
This article is a stub. You can help Hyperion Cantos Wiki by  expanding it.
According to Ummon, the Stables are a faction or party of AI's within the TechnoCore, alongside the Ultimates and the Volatiles.

The Stables, of which Ummon is a notable member,  believe that humankind has a role to play, along with TechnoCore, in the long term destiny of the galaxy.
