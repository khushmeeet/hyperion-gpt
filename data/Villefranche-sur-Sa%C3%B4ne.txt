Villefranche-sur-Saône
Villefranche-sur-Saône is the world from which Father Paul Duré came. People from this planet spoke with a particular French accent and was originally settled by people from France on Old Earth. The world is indeed named from an actual city in France. This world had oceans and featured forty-meter-tall tsunamis .
