Villefranche-sur-Saône
Villefranche-sur-Saône is the world from which Father Paul Duré came.[1] People from this planet spoke with a particular French accent and was originally settled by people from France on Old Earth.[2] The world is indeed named from an actual city in France. This world had oceans and featured forty-meter-tall tsunamis [3].
