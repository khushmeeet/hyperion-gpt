Pax Mercantilus
The Pax Mercantilus is more formally known as the Pancapitalist League of Independent Catholic Transstellar Trade Organizations.
The organization known as Opus Dei is a major competitor to the activities of the Pax Mercantilus
The Executive Council of the Pax Mercantilus consists of Kenzo Isozaki, Helvig Aron, Kennet Hay-Modhino and Anna Pelli Cognani.
