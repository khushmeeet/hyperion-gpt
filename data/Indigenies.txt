Indigenies
Within the universe of the Hyperion Cantos, the term "indigenies" refer to the Hegira colonists that became "native" to other worlds after having spent many centuries in those planets or moons. Their ancestors would have arrived at their home worlds in the original seedships. Societies that are referred to as indigenies include:

The Bikura of Hyperion
The broader human population of Hyperion
The Jewish population of New Jerusalem on Hebron
The Chitchatuk of Sol Draconi Septem
The people of the Amoiete Spectrum Helix of Vitus-Gray-Balianus B
