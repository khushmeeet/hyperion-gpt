Moneta

Moneta is a mysterious woman introduced in Fedmahn Kassad's story. Kassad first meets her in battle simulations during his FORCE training. She appears to have access to futuristic technology, such as a silver "suit" which allows the user to manipulate time.
Moneta appears from time to time and has an ambivalent sexual relation with Kassad.

True Identity
It is later revealed that Moneta is actually Rachel Weintraub, Sol Weintraub's daughter and follower of Aenea.
