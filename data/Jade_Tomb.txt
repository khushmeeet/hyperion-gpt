Jade Tomb
Overview
The Jade Tomb is one of the Time Tombs on the planet Hyperion , distinguished primarily by its emerald green color. 
At the base of the tomb, there is an entrance to Hyperion's labyrinth. 

Purpose
As of six months following the opening of the Time Tombs, the purpose of the Jade Tomb had yet to be ascertained definitively, but it appeared to have something to do with gas giant worlds. Exotic Ousters , bred to be able to live in Jovian environments arrived regularly to attempt to enter it.
