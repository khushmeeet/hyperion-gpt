Whirl
Whirl is a Jovian gas planet orbiting Barnard's Star alongside Barnard's World. It once home to the semi-sentient species called zeplins and other Jovian lifeforms (such as the Montgolfiers) who lived in the planet's cloud towers.
The Consul once held a diplomatic position on Whirl where he had a role in driving the Zeplin to extinction.
