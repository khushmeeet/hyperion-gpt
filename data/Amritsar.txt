Amritsar
Amritsar is a desert planet of the Outback. It is barely habitable, rating at the margins of the Solmev Scale. Its colonization had been recent at time of the Pax. It was settled by refugees fleeing a civil war on Parvati. It was home to a few thousand Sikhs and a few thousand Sufi. 
Aenea spent four months in Amritsar, having had been hired to build a desert community center.
