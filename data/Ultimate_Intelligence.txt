Ultimate Intelligence
The term Ultimate Intelligence (UI) refers to a hypothetical entity capable of predicting 100% of all potential outcomes in the known universe; such an intelligence is considered to be omniscient and on the level of godhood.
Two UIs are referred to in the Hyperion Cantos; the first is a hyper-powerful AI designed by the TechnoCore, whereas the second is an entity derived from the consciousnesses of all human beings.
