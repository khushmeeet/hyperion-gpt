Volatiles
This article is a stub. You can help Hyperion Cantos Wiki by  expanding it.
According to Ummon, the Volatiles are a faction or party of AI's within the TechnoCore, alongside the Stables and the Ultimates.
.
First introduced during the events of The Fall of Hyperion, the Volatiles seek the destruction of humankind, as they view the existence of humans as a hindrance to creating the Ultimate Intelligence.
