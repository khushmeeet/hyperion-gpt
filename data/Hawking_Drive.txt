Hawking Drive
The Hawking Drive is a faster-than-light propulsion system employed by starships. 
The Drive is named after esteemed 21st-century physicist Stephen Hawking, although it was derived only tangentially from his ideas.
The drive is capable of enabling travel at thousands of times faster than the speed of light.[1]
