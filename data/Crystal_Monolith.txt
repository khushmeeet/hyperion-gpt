Crystal Monolith
The Crystal Monolith is one of the Time Tombs on the planet Hyperion, distinguished from the others by its crystalline structure. 

Overview
The Crystal Monolith was sealed for much of its existence.  

Purpose
The Crystal Monolith is the final resting place for Colonel Fedmahn Kassad.
