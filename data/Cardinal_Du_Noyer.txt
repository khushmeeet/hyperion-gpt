Cardinal Du Noyer
Cardinal Du Noyer appears as an old woman in perfect health, with dark clear eyes, minimal wrinkles, white hair cut almost to the scalp, and firm cheekbones. 
She holds the office of prefect and president of Cor Unum and defacto spokesperson for the private agency of Opus Dei. She is explicitly identified as not one of the administrators of Opus Dei.[1]
