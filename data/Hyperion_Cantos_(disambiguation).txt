Hyperion Cantos (disambiguation)
In the Hyperion universe, the Hyperion Cantos are an epic poem written by the character Martin Silenus. For this usage, see Hyperion Cantos (Martin Silenus).
Outside the Hyperion universe, the phrase is occasionally used to refer to the four novels by Dan Simmons that are set in the Hyperion universe. For this usage, view Hyperion Cantos on Wikipedia.
