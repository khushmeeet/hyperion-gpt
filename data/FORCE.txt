FORCE
FORCE is the collective name for the armed forces of the Hegemony of Man. The headquarters are located on Tau Ceti Center. The Olympos Command School, a famous FORCE academy, is situated on Olympos Mons on Mars.
FORCE has different branches, named according to the primary environment in which they fight. The four main branches are as follows:

FORCE:space
FORCE:marine
FORCE:navy
FORCE:ground
When the various branches fight together, they are referred to as FORCE:combined.
