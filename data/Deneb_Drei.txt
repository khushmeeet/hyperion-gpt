Deneb Drei
Deneb Drei is a planet that was part of the WorldWeb and of the River Tethys circuit. [1] It was home to Corporal Bassin Kee and the Jamnu Republic.[2] Sol Weintraub farcast to this planet in search of a Shrike temple that would assist his daughter Rachel Weintraub's in her sickness.[3]
