Weaponry
Various types of weapons technology are employed by the Hegemony, Pax, Ousters and other factions.

Weapon Classes by Projectile Type
Bhee-keepers - A weapon that fires beams of high-energy electrons.
CPBs - Ship-based particle beam weapons.
Deathwands - Highly advanced energy weapons that kill human targets to the exclusion of all other objects in the line of fire.
Flechette launchers
Hawking Missiles - Missiles equipped with their own Hawking Drives.
Hellwhips - Weaponized laser technology, ranging from handheld laser pistols to vehicle-mounted emitters.
Plasma explosives - Various types of grenade and bomb employ plasma to destroy targets.
Stunners
